# Deploying YIMS Website to Replit

## Steps to Deploy (100% FREE - No Payment)

### 1. Go to Replit
- Open https://replit.com
- Sign in/Sign up (free)

### 2. Create New Replit
- Click **"Create"** button
- Select **"Import from GitHub"**
- Paste: `sandeshvengala/yims-website`
- Click **"Import"**

### 3. Replit Auto-Deploys
- Replit automatically:
  - Installs dependencies (`npm install`)
  - Builds your app
  - Starts the servers
  - Gives you a public URL like: `https://yims-website.replit.dev`
